
=============================================

隐藏Linux的版本信息

[root@bestjiansong ~]# cat /etc/issue
CentOS release 6.3 (Final)
Kernel \r on an \m

[root@bestjiansong ~]# cat /etc/issue.net 
CentOS release 6.3 (Final)
Kernel \r on an \m

清空版本信息
> /etc/issue

或者
[root@bestjiansong ~]# cat /dev/null > /etc/issue


可以修改 /etc/motd 文件
